📌 **Frequently Asked Questions**

**Q: What is Eleusis FX?**
A: We're a UK-based prop firm evaluation service. Professional traders pass your prop firm challenge (FTMO, The5ers, etc.) on your behalf. You keep the funded account and profit splits.

**Q: What's your pass rate?**
A: 87% across all evaluations. We're transparent about this — it's not 100% because markets have risk. But 87% is industry-leading.

**Q: How much does it cost?**
A: Pricing depends on the account size and prop firm. Visit https://eleusisfx.uk for current pricing. You also pay the prop firm's challenge fee directly.

**Q: Is this legal?**
A: Yes. It's a service contract under UK law — no different from hiring any professional to perform a skilled task. Full details: https://eleusisfx.uk/articles/can-someone-else-trade-your-prop-firm-evaluation

**Q: Which prop firms do you support?**
A: FTMO, The5ers, FundedNext, E8 Funding, TopStep, and most major prop firms. If your firm isn't listed, ask us — we likely support it.

**Q: How long does it take?**
A: Typically 3-5 weeks for both phases combined. Depends on market conditions and the specific prop firm's rules.

**Q: Do I need trading experience?**
A: No. We handle all the trading. You just need to purchase the evaluation and share login credentials.

**Q: What happens after I'm funded?**
A: The funded account is yours. You trade it yourself, or we can discuss ongoing management. Profit splits go directly to you from the prop firm.

**Q: How do I get started?**
A: Apply at https://eleusisfx.uk or DM an admin here on Discord.

**Q: Can I see your track record?**
A: Yes — check our website for verified results, or visit your client dashboard at https://eleusisfx.uk/dashboard if you're already a client.
