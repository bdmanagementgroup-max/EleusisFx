# Recommended Discord Channel Structure

## 📢 INFORMATION
- **#welcome** — Server rules, about Eleusis FX, how to get started
- **#announcements** — Company news, new articles, service updates
- **#faq** — Frequently asked questions about prop firm evaluations

## 📚 EDUCATION
- **#articles** — Full article summaries from eleusisfx.uk
- **#daily-tips** — Quick daily trading insights
- **#prop-firm-guides** — Detailed guides on FTMO, The5ers, FundedNext, etc.
- **#strategy** — Trading strategy discussions

## 📊 MARKET ANALYSIS
- **#daily-analysis** — Daily forex & crypto market analysis (automated)
- **#trading-signals** — Signal alerts from the trading analysis agent
- **#economic-calendar** — Key upcoming economic events

## 💬 COMMUNITY
- **#general** — General chat
- **#trade-ideas** — Share your trade setups
- **#results** — Post your funded results / progress

## 🔒 CLIENTS ONLY (role-gated)
- **#client-updates** — Private updates for active evaluation clients
- **#client-support** — Direct support channel
