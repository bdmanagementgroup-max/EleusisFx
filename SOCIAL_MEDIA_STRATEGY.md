1. **Educational Content** (40%) - Trading concepts, prop firm explanations, risk management
2. **Market Analysis** (25%) - Weekly outlook, key levels, economic event previews
3. **Client Success Stories** (15%) - Anonymous case studies, testimonials, results
4. **Behind-the-Scenes** (10%) - Team insights, company culture, process transparency
5. **Promotional/Service** (10%) - Service offerings, free guides, special announcements

- **Monday**: Market outlook (all platforms) + Engagement question (Facebook/LinkedIn)
- **Tuesday**: Educational content (LinkedIn focus) + Article promotion
- **Wednesday**: Quick tips/tutorials (TikTok focus) + Mid-week analysis
- **Thursday**: Client success/behind-scenes (Facebook focus) + Myth busting
- **Friday**: Week-end recap + CTA (all platforms)
- **Weekend**: Community engagement + Discussion starters
