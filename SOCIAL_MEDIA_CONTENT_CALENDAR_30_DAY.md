### Compliance Guidelines
1. No guaranteed returns or profit promises
2. Include risk disclaimer where appropriate: "Trading involves risk"
3. No financial advice disclaimer on analysis: "For educational purposes only"
4. Client testimonials require written permission
5. Follow FCA guidelines for financial promotions
6. Disclose any partnerships or sponsorships
7. Keep records of all financial promotions for 6 years
