**Escalation:** Negative mentions → CMO within 2 hours

### Phase 2: Engagement Protocols (Week 2)

#### Response Guidelines
**Positive Mentions:**
- Like/react within 1 hour
- Comment with appreciation + value-add within 4 hours
- Share/user-generated content with permission + credit
- Follow user if relevant industry account

**Questions/Inquiries:**
- Acknowledge within 1 hour
- Provide helpful answer within 4 hours
- Direct to relevant resource (guide, calculator, comparison)
- Invite to DM for detailed discussion if needed

**Negative Feedback/Criticism:**
- Acknowledge within 1 hour (public if public comment)
- Move to DM within 2 hours for resolution
- Never delete or hide legitimate criticism
- Follow up after resolution to ensure satisfaction
- Log for internal improvement tracking

**Spam/Inappropriate:**
- Hide/report immediately
- Do not engage
- Document for pattern tracking

#### Engagement Targets (Daily)
- **Comments:** Respond to 100% of comments within 4 business hours
- **DMs/Messages:** Respond to 100% within 2 business hours  
- **Mentions:** Acknowledge 100% of brand mentions within 1 hour
- **Proactive Engagement:** Initiate 10+ conversations/day with relevant accounts
- **Community Participation:** Join 2+ industry discussions/day

### Phase 3: Reporting & Optimization (Ongoing)

#### Weekly Metrics to Track
**Growth Metrics:**
- New followers (platform breakdown)
- Follower growth rate (% weekly)
- Reach/impressions (unique accounts seeing content)
- Profile visits
- Website clicks from social (UTM tracked)

**Engagement Metrics:**
- Engagement rate (likes+comments+shares)/impressions
- Average comments per post
- Average shares per post
- Click-through rate on links
- Profile visit-to-follower conversion rate

**Sentiment Metrics:**
- Positive vs negative vs neutral mentions
- Compliment-to-complaint ratio
- Average response sentiment (from our replies)
- Trending topics in our conversations

**Lead Attribution:**
- Social-sourced leads (UTM + form tracking)
- Conversion rate: social visitor → lead
- Cost per social lead (time investment)
- Lead quality assessment (sales team feedback)

#### Optimization Process
**Monthly (1st week):**
1. Review previous month's data
2. Identify top-performing content types/topics
3. Identify underperforming areas
4. Adjust content calendar for upcoming month
5. Test new formats or platforms as needed

**Weekly (Every Monday):**
1. Review past week performance
2. Note what worked/didn't work
3. Adjust upcoming week's content if needed
4. Ensure all scheduled content is created/approved

#### Compliance & Safety
**Financial Promotion Guidelines (FCA/UK):**
- Never guarantee returns or profits
- Always include risk disclaimer where appropriate: "Trading involves risk of loss"
- Avoid language suggesting easy money or get-rich-quick
- Clearly distinguish educational content from financial advice
- Include disclaimer: "For educational purposes only. Not financial advice."
- Keep records of all financial promotions for minimum 6 years

**Platform-Specific Rules:**
- Follow each platform's community guidelines
- Respect copyright (use only owned or licensed content)
- Disclose any partnerships or sponsorships
- Follow platform-specific rules for financial content
- Monitor for and report impersonation accounts

**Data Privacy:**
- Never share client information without explicit written consent
- Anonymize all case studies and testimonials
- Follow GDPR for any data collected via social media
- Securely store any collected data
