# Eleusis FX Cross-Promotion Plan: Social Media ↔ Telegram/Discord
## For ELE-61: Social Media Revival Plan

### Current State Analysis
**Existing Channels:**
- Telegram: @eleusisfxofficial (active, trading signals focus)
- Discord: eleusisfx server (community, analysis, support)
- Email Newsletter: Sent bi-weekly to subscribers
- Website: eleusisfx.uk (main conversion hub)

**Social Media Targets (ELE-61):**
- Facebook: New page to establish
- LinkedIn: New company page to establish  
- TikTok: New account to establish
- Google Business: Profile to optimize

### Cross-Promotion Strategy

#### Phase 1: Launch Integration (Weeks 1-2)
**From Social → Existing Channels:**
1. **Profile Bios:** All social profiles include:
   - "Join our FREE trading community: Telegram + Discord"
   - Links: t.me/eleusisfxofficial | discord.gg/eleusisfx
   - Update weekly with current subscriber/member counts

2. **Pinned Posts:** Each platform features:
   - "Welcome! Get our free trading guide + join 2,000+ traders in our community"
   - Image: Screenshot of Discord/Telegram with member count
   - Link: eleusisfx.uk/#free-guide (landing page with social proof)

3. **Content Integration:**
   - Every 4th post: Community highlight ("This week in our Discord...")
   - Share Telegram signal performance (anonymized) as social proof
   - Promote Discord voice chats/AMAs in advance
   - Share user-generated content from community (with permission)

**From Existing Channels → Social:**
1. **Telegram:**
   - Weekly: "New post on our [Facebook/LinkedIn/TikTok] - [Topic]"
   - Signal posts: Add "Follow us on social for more analysis: [links]"
   - Monthly: "Which social platform should we focus on next? Vote in Discord"

2. **Discord:**
   - #announcements: "We're live on [Platform]! Go follow: [link]"
   - #daily-analysis: Share snippets of social content as discussion starters
   - #resources: Pin social media guides and content calendars
   - Monthly event: "Social media AMA - ask us about our strategy"

3. **Email Newsletter:**
   - Header: "Follow us on our new social channels!"
   - Section: "This week on social:" + 3-item roundup with links
   - Footer: Social icons with links to all profiles
   - Dedicated issue: "Our social media strategy & how you can benefit"

#### Phase 2: Integrated Campaigns (Weeks 3-6)

**Campaign 1: "Trader Journey Series"**
- **Discord/Telegram:** Share member progress stories (weekly)
- **Social Media:** Create polished versions as case studies/video testimonials
- **Cross-promote:** "See full journey in our community" → link to Discord
- **CTA:** "Want to be featured? Share your progress in #progress-channel"

**Campaign 2: "Weekly Analysis Dual-Release"**
- **Telegram:** Early morning signal/analysis (6-7 AM GMT)
- **Discord:** Live analysis session (7-8 PM GMT) 
- **Social Media:** Recap posts (next day): "Yesterday's key levels + what happened"
- **Engagement:** "What did YOU trade? Share results in Discord/Telegram"

**Campaign 3: "Social-Only Challenges"**
- **Monthly:** "30-Day Consistency Challenge" announced on social
- **Tracking:** Participants report daily in Discord #challenge-channel
- **Rewards:** Featured in social media, Discord role, small prize
- **Cross-promote:** Daily reminders in all channels

**Campaign 4: "User-Generated Content Month"**
- **Prompt:** "Show us your trading setup + morning routine"
- **Platform:** TikTok/Instagram Reels (primary), Facebook/LinkedIn (secondary)
- **Incentive:** Best 5 get featured everywhere + 1:1 coaching session
- **Community:** Discord channel for submissions and voting

#### Phase 3: Automation & Scaling (Ongoing)

**Content Repurposing Workflow:**
```
Long-form Content (Blog/Guide)
        ↓
LinkedIn Article (800-1200 words)
        ↓
Twitter/X Thread (5-7 tweets)
        ↓
Facebook Post (summary + link)
        ↓
TikTok/IG Reel (visual concept, 30-45 sec)
        ↓
Telegram Snippet (key takeaway, 1-2 min read)
        ↓
Discord Discussion Prompt (question for community)
```

**Automated Sharing (Consider Tools):**
- **Zapier/Make.com:** New blog post → auto-share to social + Telegram
- **RSS Feed:** Website blog → Discord announcement channel
- **Buffer/Hootsuite:** Schedule social posts → auto-notify team in Discord
- **Custom Bot:** New YouTube video → post to Telegram + Discord

**Community-Generated Content Amplification:**
- **Weekly:** "Member Takeover" - let community member create content for our social
- **Monthly:** "Best Community Post" - reshare top Discord/TikTok/Telegram content
- **Quarterly:** "Community Awards" - recognize top contributors everywhere

#### Success Metrics for Cross-Promotion
**Growth Metrics:**
- Social followers originating from Telegram/Discord (UTM: social_tg, social_disc)
- Telegram growth from social referral links
- Discord growth from social invitation links
- Email subscriber growth from social CTAs

**Engagement Metrics:**
- Cross-platform engagement rate (likes/comments/shares on cross-promoted content)
- Conversion rate: social visitor → community member
- Community member → social follower rate
- Content lift: % increase in engagement when cross-promoted vs. platform-native

**Revenue/Lead Attribution:**
- Leads from social that mention Telegram/Discord in form
- Conversion improvement for users in multiple channels
- Lifetime value comparison: single-channel vs multi-channel users

### Content Guidelines for Cross-Promotion
**Voice Consistency:**
- Educational, not promotional
- Trading education focus, not financial advice
- Community-first mindset
- Transparent about what we do and don't offer

**Visual Consistency:**
- Use same color palette: #4f8ef7 (primary blue), #22c55e (accent green)
- Consistent logo usage across platforms
- Similar text overlay styles for video content
- Branded templates for quotes, stats, announcements

**Value Exchange:**
- Social → Community: Exclusive content, early access, behind-the-scenes
- Community → Social: User-generated content, testimonials, engagement signals
- Both ways: Trading education, market insights, community support
