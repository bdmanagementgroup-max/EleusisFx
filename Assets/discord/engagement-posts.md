## Engagement Post 3: Quick tip

**Channel: #risk-management**

💡 **Quick Tip: The 1% Rule**

Never risk more than 1% of your account on a single trade during an evaluation.

On a $100k FTMO account:
• 1% risk = $1,000 per trade
• Daily drawdown limit = $5,000
• That means you can take 5 full losing trades before hitting the daily limit

At 2% risk? Only 2.5 trades. One bad session and you are done.

Keep it at 1%. Always.
