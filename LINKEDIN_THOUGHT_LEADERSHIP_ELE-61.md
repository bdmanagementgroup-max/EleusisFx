## Article Templates:
Each article should include:
1. **Hook** (first 2 lines): Controversial statement, surprising stat, or relatable pain point
2. **Problem**: Clearly define the issue traders face
3. **Insight**: Our unique perspective/data/experience
4. **Solution**: Actionable advice traders can implement today
5. **Social Proof**: Brief mention of our 700+ evaluations experience
6. **Call to Action**: Clear next step (download, schedule, visit link)
7. **Hashtags**: 3-5 relevant industry tags
